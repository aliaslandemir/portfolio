About

A concise overview of my background and research direction.

Profile

I am a PhD candidate working at the interface of biophysics and biomineralization. My research explores stem cell differentiation, bone formation in hydrogels, and microfluidic technologies. I also study cell behavior on micropatterned substrates and apply deep learning to microscopy-driven data analysis.
