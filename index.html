Who am I ?

In Our Lovely Earth

I am a PhD candidate in Biophysics and Biomineralization at the University of Oslo, focusing on biophysics, biomineralization, data analysis and microfluidics. I work on a project focused on mesenchymal stem cell differentiation and mineralization in biomimetic hydrogels. Using microfluidics and modeling, I investigate ways to enhance bone formation. I also research cell behavior on micropatterned substrates. Passionate about microscopy, data analysis, and personalized medicine, I am eager to apply my skills to biotech and healthcare innovations.
