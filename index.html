Who am I?

I am a PhD candidate in Biophysics and Biomineralization at the University of Oslo. My research focuses on stem cell differentiation, bone formation in hydrogels, and microfluidic technologies. I am passionate about microscopy, data analysis, and their applications in personalized medicine.

Areas of Interest

Biomineralization: Researching calcium phosphate transformations for bone tissue engineering.
